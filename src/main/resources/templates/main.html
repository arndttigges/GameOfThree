Game of Three

The Goal is to implement a game with two independent units – the players – communicating with each other using an API.

Choose a number and play against each other to see who makes the last move. On each turn, one of the actions (+1, 0, -1) may be chosen and the number is divided by 3.

Notify other players that you want to play, accept their invitations and play with them. Your running Games are listed at the bottom of the page
